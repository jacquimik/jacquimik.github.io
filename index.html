Restyle Me



Restyle Me is a second hand clothes advertising platform aiming to connect buyers and sellers to find that perfect outfit! To get started, all you have to do is create an account and sign in. Once you have created an account, you can start uploading and sharing your second hand clothes. Once you've uploaded the item, you can search for clothes through categories that are specific to your interest and need, ultimately connecting buyers and sellers!

Sydney Sister Boutique



Sydney Sister Boutique is an ecommerce platform specialising in selling women's fashion clothing. Based off my blog, Sydney Sister encompasses many features including helpful category navigation, product information pages and an admin dashboard. Being particulary interested in e-commerce, this shortly became my favourite project. With Stripe, Facebook and Instagram integration, it became the web app that I would love to personally use in the future.

Visit Sydney Sister



Child Time Capsule



Child Time Capsule is a web app enabling parents to upload documentation of their childrens special moments and milestones in their lives. Child Time Capsule stores child information in an account made by the parent and allows them to upload photos and descriptions. With built in geolocation, the time, date & location about your childrens special moments can be stored in one safe place without the fear of losing them!
